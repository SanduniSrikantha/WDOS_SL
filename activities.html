Hop on a Train

Location
:Kandy to Ella
Price
:$34.56
Duration
:7 to 8 hours

Gear up for the most beautiful train journey as your train takes you from Kandy to the scenic, cloud-kissed town of Ella. Rivaling even the Trans-Siberian Railway, the train ride to Ella takes you through green seas of tea plantations, cottony clouds, the most breathtaking views of lush hills, and finally weaves in and out of the many mountain tunnels as it descends to reach Ella! With such a refreshing experience to offer, it is undoubtedly one of the most unique things to do in Sri Lanka.

Hot Air Balooning

Location
:Kandalma
Price
:$288 onwards
Duration
:1 hour 30 minutes

Watch the beautiful lushes of Sri Lanka from an exclusive location! Hot Air Ballooning in Kandalama is an amazing amalgamation of thrills and serenity, as you will be witnessing beautiful landscapes while ballooning in vibrant hot air balloons floating in the clouds. This is something you must not miss out on while vacationing in Sri Lanka. Whether holidaying with friends or family, this one excursion will make you witness something majestic and surreal.

White Water Rafting

Location
:Kithulgala
Price
:$28 onwards (depending on group size)
Duration
:1 hour

One of the best things to do in Sri Lanka is white water rafting at the Kelani river in Kitulgala. Kitualgala experiences the highest amount of rainfall and hence a place where water tides are high enough to try adventurous rafting. You will find grade three rapids here for beginners and intermediate rafters.

Price

Duration

Sri Lanka is a great tourist spot for adventure seekers. The clear waters make for great diving spots. It is one of the activities that attract tourists from all across the globe. Nilaveli is an amazing dive site to watch coral reefs.
